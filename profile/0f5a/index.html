Mr. Andrew Boag (Catalyst IT) bio
Andrew Boag is a child of the 70s, straight out of Palmerston North in the lower north island of friendly kiwiana. After years of school and tertiary education (Otago Comp Sci Bachelors), he headed out into the world and ended up working in Moscow from 1998 to 2005 in various IT roles from Media to Banking. Yes he speaks Russian, and yes he drinks vodka (not so often any more thankfully) Andrew arrived back in NZ in 2005 and started working for Catalyst IT in the Telecoms team where NZ was storming ahead with various internet delivery and SMS gateway applications. 2007 saw a change of scene and Andrew turned up in Sydney to get the Australian end of the Catalyst machine up and running. So far so good.
