Juergen Brendel (MuleSoft) bio
Juergen Brendel works with MuleSoft, makers of the popular Mule ESB. While Juergen's background is in low-level network processing and traffic management, in recent years he has worked in the web development and data integration space. Over the years Juergen has been a software developer, architect, engineering manager, CTO and a long-time user and advocate for FOSS. He also had the opportunity to give presentations and guest lectures about network security and FOSS at a number of conferences and universities. Juergen has lived and worked in a number of positions and companies in Europe and North America, but currently lives with his family in New Zealand. Dealing with an extreme case of tele-commuting, he works for clients around the world.
